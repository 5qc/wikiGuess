How To Play:

The game will provide you with an image (or more). Your goal is to type in the box what you think the wikiHow article these images are referencing. If you get it right, you'll get a point. If you get it wrong, you'll get no points.

The game goes on for an infinite amount of time, and you can finish the game whenever. To do that, click the button at the top right that says "Stop". Upon clicking that, the results of the game will show.

And that's the basics of the game. Now enjoy! :)
